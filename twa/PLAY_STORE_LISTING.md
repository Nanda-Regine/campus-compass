## Full Description

**VarsityOS is the operating system for South African student life.**

Not just an app — a complete intelligence system that follows you through your day: from your morning taxi commute to late-night exam prep, from NSFAS budgeting to mental health support.

Built by South Africans, for South Africans. Ubuntu is in every feature.

---

**🧠 NOVA AI — Your 24/7 Study & Life Companion**
Chat with Nova, your AI companion trained on South African student life. Get instant help with calculus, essay structure, NSFAS questions, budgeting advice, or just someone to talk to at 2am. Nova knows SA universities, SA funding, SA challenges.
