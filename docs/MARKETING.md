## 1. The one-liner

**A student from Sandton arrives at university with a private tutor, a financial advisor, a life coach, a career mentor, and a personal assistant. A student from Soweto arrives with a feature phone and a dream.**

VarsityOS is the equaliser — an AI team every student deserves, in a single beautifully designed, offline-first, 11-language app that works on a Tecno Spark with 2GB of prepaid data.

Not a study planner. Not a budgeting app. A **life operating system** spanning nine domains: Mind, Body, Money, Safety, Movement, Growth, Community, Work, and Future.

---

## 2. The problem we exist to solve

South Africa has one of the most unequal education systems in the world. Over a million students are enrolled across 26 public universities — the majority first-generation, NSFAS-funded, commuting long distances on minibus taxis, studying through load shedding, rationing prepaid data by the megabyte.

The support that wealthier students take for granted simply isn't there:

- No tutor for the calculus they're stuck on at 11pm.
- No financial advisor to warn them they'll run out of NSFAS money before month-end.
- No one to tell them about the bursary they already qualify for.
- No mentor for the first job. No safety net for the 2am panic attack.

The result is a dropout crisis and a wasted generation of talent — not for lack of ability, but for lack of scaffolding.

**VarsityOS closes that gap.** Every performance optimisation, every offline feature, every multilingual string, every crisis-detection threshold is built for one student we call **Nomvula** — 19, first in her family at university, commuting two hours each way to Wits, on NSFAS, brilliant, determined, and alone with her calculus at midnight. When we debate a decision, we ask one question: *does this serve Nomvula?*

## 3. What VarsityOS is

A **Progressive Web App** (installable on Android/iOS, no app-store gatekeeping) that unifies the entire student experience into interconnected "OS" rooms. It is:

- **Comprehensive** — 72 screens across academics, money, health, safety, transport, career, and community.
- **Intelligent** — Nova, an AI companion grounded in SA-specific context, plus a cross-domain intelligence layer that reads signals across rooms (mood → burnout risk, spend → NSFAS runway, sleep → study velocity).
- **Made for the reality** — NSFAS-native, load-shedding-aware, taxi-fare-literate, data-frugal, and available in all 11 official languages.
- **Offline-first** — the safety timer, saved data, and recently visited rooms work with no signal at all.
- **Ubuntu by design** — shared notes, peer tutoring, study twins, stokvels, mutual aid: your success and mine are not separate things.

## 4. The full feature map

Rooms are grouped the way students think — by area of life. Below is the real, shipped surface (not a roadmap).

### 🧠 Academic
- **StudyOS** — an 18-tab command centre: Tasks, Calendar, Timetable, Exams, Grades, Flashcards (FSRS spaced repetition), Modules, Focus (Pomodoro), Habits, Graduation Audit, Attendance, Study Velocity, Study Pods, Past Papers, Smart Grade Forecaster, Module Orbit Map, and an AI Study Kit generator — plus an anti-procrastination toolkit (Body Double, Just Start, Commitment Contracts, Procrastination Profiler, Anti-Spiral Recovery).
- **Notes marketplace**, **peer tutoring**, **study groups**, **textbook marketplace**, **document reader** (PDF/DOCX extraction), and **LMS sync** (Moodle / Blackboard / Canvas).

### 💰 Money
- **BudgetOS** — 11 tabs: Overview, Expenses, Wallet, Fees, **NSFAS**, **Data budget**, AI Coach, Appeal writer, Credit score, Money 101, Stokvel.
- **NSFAS Tracker** — disbursements, allowance runway, the N+ rule, appeals, document vault.
- **Bursary finder** (Funza, Sasol, Eskom, Transnet, Old Mutual…), **digital stokvels**, **student tax guidance**, **SASSA/SRD guide**, and a **student discount directory**.

### ❤️ Body & Mind
- **Health hub**, **menstrual cycle tracker**, **sexual health hub**, **sleep module**, **fitness tracker**.
- **MealsOS** — AI meal planner, weekly plans, grocery lists, SA recipes under R50, nutrition, Kitchen 101, food-spend tracker, and a **Food Insecurity Mode** for the hardest weeks.
- **Regulate** — emotional-regulation and mindfulness sessions; Nova crisis detection wired to SA hotlines (SADAG, Lifeline).

### 🛡️ Safety
- **SafetyOS** — SOS panic button (SA emergency numbers), **Walk Me Home** offline check-in timer, **Safety Map** (incident hotspots + nearest police/hospital/pharmacy + load-shedding warnings), self-defence library, emergency contacts, incident reporting, legal rights, GBV response guide.
- **Guardian Access** — a trusted contact can watch a student get home via a secure token link.

### 🚕 Movement
- **MovementOS** — Mapbox route planning, saved routes, a **Lift Club** for shared rides, transport tips, and a commute planner. Understands minibus taxis, MyCiTi/BRT, Uber/Bolt, campus shuttles, and long-distance minibuses — with real SA fare ranges and a cheapest-vs-fastest comparison.

### 🚀 Career & Growth
- **CareerOS** — AI CV builder, AI mock interviews, skills-gap analysis, SA job board, application tracker, alumni **mentor network**, LinkedIn bootcamp, and graduate-outcomes data.
- **Skills academy**, **entrepreneurship/side-hustle OS**, **part-time work & shift tracker**, **first-gen toolkit**, and a graduate **Launch Pad**.

### 🤝 Community
- **SocialOS** — 9 tabs: campus feed, "who's around now" presence (with a live map), focus rooms, events, clubs/societies, study twins, accountability partners, **mutual aid**, and community wisdom.
- **Campus marketplace**, **civic education** (voting & rights), **SRC analytics**, and an **international student hub**.

### 🏠 Life
- **HousingOS** — accommodation finder for the student rental reality.

## 5. Nova — the AI companion

Nova is a companion, not a replacement for community — AI that amplifies human bonds rather than substituting them.

- **Dual-model architecture** — **Anthropic Claude (Sonnet)** for depth (tutoring, coaching, crisis conversations) with prompt caching for cost efficiency; **Groq (Llama 3.1)** for fast, cheap tasks (task decomposition, study-kit and recipe generation).
- **Grounded in SA context** — a bespoke knowledge base for NSFAS mechanics, bursaries, SA academic conventions, and local resources — not generic global data.
- **Tutors across ~13 subjects** — Calculus, Statistics, Chemistry, Physics, Accounting, Economics, Law, Computer Science, Biology, Academic Writing, and more.
- **Crisis-aware** — detects distress and responds with warmth, resources, and a path to real human support (SADAG, campus counselling).
- **Proactive** — daily briefs, catch-up planners, weekly insight reports, budget receipt scanning (OCR), and graduation optimisation.

## 7. The retention engine

A gamification system engineered for a **four-year journey**, not a 30-day streak:

- **9 levels** — Fresher 🌱 → Survivor 💪 → Grinder ⚡ → Scholar 📚 → Pioneer 🚀 → Trailblazer 🌠 → Legend 🏆 → Graduate 🎓 → Alumni 🌟 (0 → 100,000 XP).
- **40+ tracked XP events** with per-day caps that reward consistency, not grinding.
- **5 domains with Domain Flames** — separate 7- and 30-day streaks for Academic, Money, Life, Career and Community.
- **Compound Day** — touch 3+ domains in one day for a +200 XP burst.
- **11 Archetypes** (Ubuntu Graduate, Scholar-Builder, The Hustler, Community Heart…) computed from a rolling 7-day profile — identity, not just points.
- **~45 badges**, **~30 daily challenges**, and a weighted **Mystery Box** loot table (XP jackpots, multipliers, Domain Shields).
- **VarsityOS Score** — a 0–1000 composite that, true to Ubuntu, counts **wellbeing and community contribution** alongside academics (Academic 400 / Wellness 300 / Career 200 / Social 100).

## 8. Engineering

Production-grade, mobile-first, and tuned relentlessly for low-end devices and expensive data.

**Stack**
- **Next.js 14 (App Router)**, React 18, TypeScript 5 — thin server components seeding client "OS" rooms.
- **Supabase (PostgreSQL)** with Row-Level Security across **82 migrations**; SSR auth middleware.
- **Zustand** state; **Tailwind CSS**; **Framer Motion**.
- **Mapbox GL** for all mapping (cached tiles + geocoding for data savings).
- **Vercel** hosting with Analytics & Speed Insights.

**AI** — Anthropic Claude + Groq, with tiered usage limits and prompt caching.

**Resilience & security**
- **Offline-first PWA** (Workbox): NetworkFirst pages, CacheFirst assets/tiles, an offline write queue, and a `/offline` fallback. Installable as a TWA on Android.
- **Arcjet** bot protection, **Upstash Redis** rate limiting, strict **CSP/HSTS** headers, **web-push + Firebase FCM** notifications.
- **Sentry** error monitoring, **PostHog** product analytics, **Inngest** background jobs.
- Full **POPIA/PAIA** compliance (consent gating, privacy/terms/PAIA pages, no dark patterns).

**Performance for the real world** — AVIF/WebP images, route-level code splitting, aggressive caching, and Data Saver Mode throughout.

**Quality** — Vitest + happy-dom unit tests and Playwright, with a first suite of pure-function tests around the XP engine.

## 9. Research & evidence base

VarsityOS is evidence-informed, not vibes-driven:

- **Spaced repetition** — flashcards run on **FSRS** (Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler), a modern, research-backed successor to SM-2, for durable long-term recall.
- **CBT-based support** — Nova's mental-health scaffolding draws on cognitive-behavioural techniques, paired with hard crisis-detection thresholds and SA hotline hand-offs.
- **Behavioural design** — variable-reward mystery boxes, implementation intentions, commitment contracts, and body-doubling are lifted directly from the procrastination and habit-formation literature.
- **The SA context is the research** — design decisions are anchored in the documented realities of first-generation students, NSFAS funding mechanics, prepaid-data poverty, load shedding, and long informal-transport commutes. We optimise for a 2GB/month device because that is the median, not the exception.
- **Whole-person model** — the intelligence layer correlates wellness, sleep, spend and study signals because a hungry, stressed, financially anxious student cannot learn; we measure and support the whole human.

## 10. Impact — Ubuntu in the product

VarsityOS is built through eight Ubuntu principles:

1. **Ukwabelana (Sharing)** — shared notes, past papers and bursary discoveries flow back to the community.
2. **Ukuhlonipha (Respect)** — all 11 official languages, first-class.
3. **Ukuzimela (Self-reliance through community)** — peer tutoring lets students earn by helping others.
4. **Ubuntu in crisis** — no student faces the dark alone; crisis detection meets a 2am panic with warmth and a real path to help.
5. **Ukuphila (Wholeness)** — wellness, meals and financial-health scoring alongside academics.
6. **Isimilo (Character over credentials)** — the score counts wellbeing and contribution, not just marks.
7. **Ukuxhumaneka (Connectedness)** — technology that binds rather than isolates.
8. **Imvula-mlomo (Voice to the voiceless)** — offline-first, data-frugal, WhatsApp-reachable.

**Success looks like:** a rural-university student accessing the same support as a Sandton graduate; NSFAS students who never run dry by surprise; a student who was going to drop out staying because Nova was there at 2am; a first-generation graduate landing their first job and crediting the skills they built here; a national student knowledge commons. **When Nomvula graduates, ubuntu wins.**

## 11. Business model

Freemium, priced for the SA student wallet, billed in ZAR via **Paystack** (recurring, webhook-verified):

| Plan | Price | Nova messages |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | R0 / month | Generous free tier |
| **Scholar** | ~R39 / month | Expanded Nova + premium tools |
| **Premium** | ~R79 / month | Highest Nova limits |

The core life-safety and academic tools stay free forever — monetisation is on AI depth and power-user features, never on locking a struggling student out of budgeting, safety, or NSFAS tracking. Additional upside: **referrals** (earn Nova credits), and **institutional** partnerships (broadcasts, SRC analytics, institution onboarding).
